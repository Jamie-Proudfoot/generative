Notes

1. Ground states were computed in pm6 (toluene) but transition states were in pm6 (water)

2. Nitro-Michael and Aza-Michael reactions have different atom numbering indices

3. The HDF5 files are unix-ordered! (e.g. 1, 10, 100, 1000, 101, 102, etc.)

4. Core-atom indexing differs between aza-Michael and nitro-Michael datasets:

nitro-michael
O C CA CB
4 3 2 1

aza-michael
O C CA CB
3 2  1  4
